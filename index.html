Our Story

Bringing the authentic taste of Filipino cuisine to your table since 2005

A Taste of the Philippines Since 2005

Founded by culinary expert Ms. Catherine "Cathy" Sergio, our restaurant has been serving authentic Filipino cuisine for over 15 years. Our philosophy is simple: use the finest ingredients, prepare them with passion, and serve with the warmth of Filipino hospitality.

Every dish tells a story of tradition, family recipes, and Ms. Cathy's dedication to sharing Filipino culinary excellence. We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, while importing specialty items directly from the Philippines to ensure authentic flavors.

Ms. Cathy learned her cooking skills from her grandmother in Quezon City, a city known as the culinary capital of the Philippines. Her journey from home cook to restaurateur is a testament to her passion for Filipino food and culture.
